Venue & Travel

Join us in Bangkok, Thailand

Conference Venue

Impact Muangthong Thani

Located in Nonthaburi, Bangkok's neighboring province, Impact Muangthong Thani is Thailand's premier exhibition and convention center, offering world-class facilities and easy accessibility.

Address

99 Popular Road, Banmai Subdistrict, Pakkret District, Nonthaburi 11120, Thailand

Contact

+66 2 833 4444

Website

www.impact.co.th
View on Google Maps

Facilities: Impact Muangthong Thani features state-of-the-art meeting rooms, exhibition halls, on-site restaurants, ample parking, and high-speed internet access throughout the venue.

Getting There

Suvarnabhumi Airport

(BKK)

By Taxi/Car

Approximately 40-60 minutes depending on traffic. Take the expressway via Chaeng Watthana Road. Fare: ~500-700 THB.

By Airport Rail Link + Taxi

Take Airport Rail Link to Phaya Thai, then taxi to venue (~60-90 minutes total). More economical option.

Don Mueang Airport

(DMK)

By Taxi/Car

Approximately 20-30 minutes. Direct route via Chaeng Watthana Road. Fare: ~200-300 THB. Closest airport to the venue.

By Public Transport

Take bus or train to nearby stations, then taxi to venue. Less convenient but budget-friendly.

Tip: Don Mueang Airport (DMK) is significantly closer to Impact Muangthong Thani. If you have flight options, DMK may be more convenient.
