FAPA 2026 Congress Website Officially Launched!

We are thrilled to announce the official launch of the FAPA 2026 Congress website! The 27th FAPA Congress will be held from November 3-7, 2026, at Impact Muangthong Thani, Nonthaburi, Thailand.

Under the theme "Advancing Pharmacy through Innovation, Collaboration, and Sustainability," we invite pharmacists, researchers, and healthcare professionals worldwide to join us in this prestigious event.
