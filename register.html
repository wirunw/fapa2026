Registration FAQs

Can I change my registration category after payment?

Yes, please contact us and we'll help you modify your registration. Additional fees may apply if upgrading.

What is included in the student registration?

Student registration includes the same benefits as regular registration. Valid student ID must be presented at registration desk.

Can I register on-site?

Yes, on-site registration will be available, but rates will be higher. We recommend registering online in advance.

Will I receive a certificate of attendance?

Yes, all registered participants will receive a certificate of attendance after the congress.

Are continuing education credits available?

Information about continuing education credits will be announced closer to the congress date.
