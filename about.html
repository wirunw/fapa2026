About FAPA

The Federation of Asian Pharmaceutical Associations (FAPA) is the premier regional organization dedicated to advancing the pharmaceutical profession across Asia.

Founded to promote collaboration and excellence in pharmacy practice, education, and research, FAPA brings together pharmaceutical associations from across the Asian continent.

Our Mission

To enhance pharmaceutical care and public health across Asia through professional development, knowledge exchange, and collaborative initiatives that support pharmacists in their vital role in healthcare systems.

Our Vision

To be the leading voice for pharmaceutical excellence in Asia, fostering innovation, sustainability, and collaboration among pharmaceutical professionals for improved patient outcomes and community health.

Key Activities

  • Organizing biennial congresses to facilitate knowledge sharing
  • Supporting pharmaceutical education and research initiatives
  • Promoting professional standards and best practices
  • Strengthening regional collaboration and partnerships

About PAT

The Pharmacy Association of Thailand (PAT) under Royal Patronage of His Majesty the King is the national professional organization representing pharmacists throughout Thailand.

Established to promote the pharmaceutical profession and protect public health, PAT serves as the voice of Thai pharmacists and works to elevate pharmacy practice standards across the nation.

Our Mission

To advance the pharmaceutical profession in Thailand by supporting pharmacists' professional development, promoting pharmaceutical care excellence, and advocating for policies that enhance medication safety and public health.

Our Vision

To be Thailand's leading professional pharmaceutical organization, recognized for our commitment to pharmaceutical excellence, innovation in practice, and dedication to improving healthcare outcomes for all Thai citizens.

Core Responsibilities

  • Continuing education and professional development programs
  • Setting and maintaining professional practice standards
  • Advocacy for pharmacists' rights and professional interests
  • Collaboration with government and healthcare organizations

Royal Patronage: PAT is honored to operate under the Royal Patronage of His Majesty the King, reflecting the importance of the pharmaceutical profession in Thai society.
