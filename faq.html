Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

General Information

When and where will FAPA 2026 be held?

The 27th FAPA Congress will be held from November 3-7, 2026, at Impact Muangthong Thani, Nonthaburi, Thailand.

What is the theme of FAPA 2026?

The theme is "Advancing Pharmacy through Innovation, Collaboration, and Sustainability"

Who can attend FAPA 2026?

FAPA 2026 is open to pharmacists, pharmaceutical scientists, researchers, students, and professionals in related healthcare fields from around the world.

Registration

When does registration open?

Registration will open in early 2026. Please check our website regularly for updates.

What are the registration fees?

Registration fees will be announced soon. There will be early bird discounts and special rates for students.

What is included in the registration fee?

Registration includes access to all scientific sessions, exhibition area, congress materials, coffee breaks, and welcome reception. Gala dinner may require separate registration.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my registration?

Cancellation policies will be announced with registration details. Generally, refunds may be subject to administrative fees depending on the cancellation date.

Abstract Submission

When is the deadline for abstract submission?

Abstract submission deadline will be announced soon. Please check the Call for Abstracts page for updates.

What types of presentations are available?

Abstracts can be submitted for oral presentations or poster presentations. The scientific committee will make the final decision on presentation format.

How will I know if my abstract is accepted?

Notification of acceptance will be sent via email. You can also check your submission status through the online portal.

Travel & Accommodation

Do I need a visa to visit Thailand?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Please check with the Royal Thai Embassy or Consulate in your country. We can provide invitation letters for visa applications upon request.

Are there official congress hotels?

Yes, we will have official congress hotels with special rates. Hotel information will be available on the Venue & Travel page.

How do I get from the airport to the venue?

Impact Muangthong Thani is approximately 30-40 minutes from Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) and 45-60 minutes from Don Mueang Airport (DMK) by taxi. Detailed transportation information will be provided on our website.

Other Questions

Will there be CME/CPD credits?

Yes, continuing education credits will be available for participants. Details will be announced closer to the congress date.

Is there a mobile app for the congress?

Yes, we will have a congress mobile app with the full program, abstracts, and networking features. Details will be announced before the event.

Who should I contact if I have more questions?

Please visit our Contact Us page for specific department contacts, or email the secretariat at secretariat@fapa2026.com
