About FAPA

The Federation of Asian Pharmaceutical Associations (FAPA) is a regional organization dedicated to advancing pharmaceutical sciences and practice across Asia. FAPA brings together pharmaceutical associations from various Asian countries to promote collaboration, knowledge exchange, and professional development.

Through regular congresses and initiatives, FAPA aims to enhance the quality of pharmaceutical education, research, and practice while addressing common challenges faced by the profession in the region.

About PAT

The Pharmaceutical Association of Thailand (PAT) under the Royal Patronage is the leading professional organization for pharmacists in Thailand. PAT plays a crucial role in advancing pharmaceutical practice, education, and research in the country.

As the host of FAPA 2026, PAT is committed to organizing a memorable and impactful congress that showcases Thailand's rich culture and contributes to the advancement of pharmacy across Asia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FAPA 2026

Registration

When does registration open?

Online registration will open in early 2026. We will announce the exact date through our website and email notifications. Early bird rates will be available for the first period of registration.

What are the registration fees?

Registration fees will be announced soon. We will offer different rates for international participants, local participants, and students. Early bird discounts will be available for early registrants.

What is included in the registration fee?

Full registration includes access to all scientific sessions, exhibition area, congress materials, coffee breaks, lunches, welcome reception, and gala dinner. Additional social events may be available for separate purchase.

Abstract Submission

When is the deadline for abstract submission?

The abstract submission deadline will be announced when the submission portal opens. We recommend submitting early to ensure your work is considered for oral presentations.

Can I submit multiple abstracts?

Yes, you can submit multiple abstracts. However, each abstract must be registered separately, and the presenting author must be registered for the congress.

How will I know if my abstract is accepted?

All submitters will receive notification via email. Accepted abstracts will be informed whether they are selected for oral or poster presentation. Notification date will be announced with submission details.

Travel & Accommodation

Do international visitors need a visa?

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Many countries enjoy visa exemption or visa-on-arrival privileges for Thailand. Please check with the Royal Thai Embassy or Consulate in your country. We can provide invitation letters for visa applications upon request.

Are there special hotel rates for participants?

Yes, we are negotiating special rates with hotels near the venue. Information about official hotels and booking procedures will be available on our website soon. We recommend booking early as November is a popular tourist season in Thailand.

How do I get from the airport to the venue?

Impact Muangthong Thani is easily accessible from both Bangkok airports. From Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK), it's about 30 km (30-40 minutes by taxi). From Don Mueang Airport (DMK), it's about 25 km (30-40 minutes). Detailed transportation information will be provided on our website.

General Information

What is the language of the congress?

The official language of the congress is English. All presentations, proceedings, and materials will be in English.

Will there be CME/CPD credits?

Yes, we are applying for CME/CPD credits for the congress. Certificates of attendance will be provided to all registered participants. Details about credit hours will be announced closer to the event date.

Can I bring an accompanying person?

Yes, accompanying persons are welcome. Special registration packages for accompanying persons will be available, which typically include social events and optional tours. Details will be provided with registration information.
