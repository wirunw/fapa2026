Educational & Optional Tours

Explore pharmaceutical facilities and Thailand's attractions

Enhance your FAPA 2026 experience with our carefully curated educational and optional tours. Visit pharmaceutical facilities, explore Bangkok's cultural treasures, or discover Thailand's natural beauty.

Educational Tours

Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Tour

November 2, 2026 (Pre-Congress)
8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Max 30 participants

Visit a state-of-the-art pharmaceutical manufacturing facility. Tour includes GMP production areas, quality control laboratories, and discussion with technical experts about modern pharmaceutical manufacturing.

Includes:

  • • Round-trip transportation
  • • Factory tour with technical presentation
  • • Lunch
  • • Certificate of participation

Price per person

$60

Hospital Pharmacy Visit

November 2, 2026 (Pre-Congress)
9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Max 25 participants

Explore clinical pharmacy services at a leading Thai hospital. Learn about pharmacy automation, clinical pharmacy practice, and patient counseling services in Thailand's healthcare system.

Includes:

  • • Round-trip transportation
  • • Hospital pharmacy tour
  • • Clinical case discussion
  • • Lunch

Price per person

$50

Faculty of Pharmacy Visit

November 2, 2026 (Pre-Congress)
9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Max 30 participants

Visit a leading Thai Faculty of Pharmacy. Tour research laboratories, meet faculty members, and learn about pharmacy education and research programs in Thailand.

Includes:

  • • Round-trip transportation
  • • Campus and lab tours
  • • Research presentation
  • • Lunch at university

Price per person

$45

Thai Traditional Medicine Center

November 2, 2026 (Pre-Congress)
9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Max 35 participants

Discover Thai traditional medicine and herbal pharmacy. Visit an herbal medicine production facility, learn about traditional Thai remedies, and explore medicinal herb gardens.

Includes:

  • • Round-trip transportation
  • • Guided tour of herb gardens
  • • Thai massage demonstration
  • • Lunch with herbal beverages

Price per person

$55

Optional Sightseeing Tours

Explore Thailand's rich culture and beautiful destinations. Perfect for accompanying persons or post-congress relaxation.

Bangkok Highlights

Full Day

Visit Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Wat Arun, and Chao Phraya River cruise. Experience Bangkok's iconic temples and cultural landmarks.

$75

Ayutthaya Ancient City

Full Day

Explore UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visit ancient temples and ruins of Thailand's former capital with lunch included.

$70

Floating Market

Half Day
