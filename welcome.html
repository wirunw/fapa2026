Welcome Messages

Greetings from our distinguished leaders

Welcome from the LOC Chairperson

Prof.Dr. Pornsak Sriamornsak

Dear Colleagues and Friends,

On behalf of the Local Organizing Committee (LOC) and The Pharmaceutical Association of Thailand (PAT), it is my great honor and pleasure to invite you to the FAPA Congress 2026, which will be held from November 3-7, 2026, at Impact Muangthong Thani, Nonthaburi, Thailand.

Under the theme "Advancing Pharmacy through Innovation, Collaboration, and Sustainability," we have designed a comprehensive program that brings together leading experts, researchers, and practitioners from across Asia and beyond. This congress will provide an excellent platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration to address the evolving challenges and opportunities in pharmaceutical sciences.

Thailand, known for its warm hospitality and rich cultural heritage, will provide the perfect backdrop for this prestigious event. We have prepared an exciting scientific program complemented by social and cultural activities that will give you a taste of our beautiful country.

We look forward to welcoming you to Thailand and sharing an inspiring and memorable experience together.

Message from PAT President

Dr. Suvit Teerakulchon

Dear Distinguished Guests and Participants,

It is with great pride that the Pharmaceutical Association of Thailand under the Royal Patronage hosts the FAPA Congress 2026. This prestigious event represents a significant milestone for our association and the Thai pharmaceutical community.

PAT has been at the forefront of pharmaceutical development in Thailand for decades, and hosting this congress allows us to showcase our achievements while learning from our colleagues across Asia and the world. The theme of innovation, collaboration, and sustainability resonates deeply with our vision for the future of pharmacy.

We are committed to ensuring that this congress will be a memorable and productive experience for all participants. Thailand's warm hospitality combined with cutting-edge scientific discourse will create the perfect environment for professional growth and cultural exchange.

Dear Members of the FAPA Community,

The Federation of Asian Pharmaceutical Associations is delighted to hold our congress in Thailand, a country known for its rich pharmaceutical heritage and commitment to advancing healthcare across the region.

This congress embodies our mission to strengthen collaboration among Asian pharmaceutical associations, promote excellence in pharmaceutical education and practice, and contribute to improving public health outcomes throughout Asia and beyond.

I encourage all participants to actively engage in the scientific sessions, share your experiences, and build lasting professional relationships. Together, we can shape the future of pharmacy in Asia and make significant contributions to global healthcare advancement.
