Theme & Topics

Exploring the future of pharmaceutical sciences

Congress Theme

"Advancing Pharmacy through Innovation, Collaboration, and Sustainability"

About the Theme

The 27th FAPA Congress embraces a forward-thinking vision that addresses the evolving challenges and opportunities in pharmaceutical sciences. Our theme emphasizes three pillars:

Innovation

Embracing cutting-edge technologies, novel drug delivery systems, and breakthrough research in pharmaceutical sciences.

Collaboration

Fostering partnerships across academia, industry, and healthcare to advance pharmaceutical practice and patient care.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible practices, sustainable healthcare systems, and long-term solutions for global health challenges.

Scientific Topics

Pharmaceutical Sciences & Drug Discovery

Novel drug development, medicinal chemistry, pharmacology, and preclinical studies

Pharmaceutics & Drug Delivery

Formulation development, nanotechnology, targeted delivery, and controlled release systems

Clinical Pharmacy & Patient Care

Pharmaceutical care, medication therapy management, and patient safety

Pharmaceutical Analysis & Quality Assurance

Analytical methods, quality control, validation, and regulatory compliance

Biotechnology & Biopharmaceuticals

Biologics, biosimilars, gene therapy, and personalized medicine

Natural Products & Traditional Medicine

Herbal medicines, phytochemistry, and integration with modern pharmacy

Digital Health & AI in Pharmacy

Artificial intelligence, machine learning, telepharmacy, and digital therapeutics

Public Health & Pharmacy Practice

Community pharmacy, health promotion, epidemiology, and access to medicines

Pharmacy Education & Professional Development

Curriculum innovation, competency-based education, and lifelong learning

Green Pharmacy & Environmental Sustainability

Eco-friendly practices, waste management, and sustainable pharmaceutical production
