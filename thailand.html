Discover Thailand

The Land of Smiles welcomes you to FAPA 2026

Thailand, known as the "Land of Smiles," offers a unique blend of ancient traditions and modern hospitality. Prepare for your visit to Bangkok and experience Thai culture, cuisine, and warm welcome.

~70 million

Visa Information

Many nationalities can enter Thailand visa-free for tourism purposes. Please check visa requirements based on your nationality.

Visa on Arrival

Available for citizens of certain countries for stays up to 15-30 days.

Tourist Visa

Valid for 60 days, can be extended. Apply at Thai embassy in your country.

Invitation Letter

FAPA 2026 can provide an official invitation letter for visa applications.

Getting Around Bangkok

BTS & MRT

Modern sky train and metro system covering major areas of Bangkok. Clean, efficient, and air-conditioned.

Taxi & Grab

Metered taxis and ride-hailing apps (Grab) are widely available and affordable throughout Bangkok.

Airport Link

Express train from Suvarnabhumi Airport to downtown Bangkok. Fast and convenient connection.

Thai Culture & Etiquette

Do's

  • Greet with a "wai" (hands together in prayer position)
  • Remove shoes before entering temples and homes
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples
  • Show respect to the Royal Family and Buddha images

Don'ts

  • Don't touch anyone's head (considered most sacred part)
  • Don't point your feet at people or Buddha images
  • Don't raise your voice or show anger in public
  • Don't step over people or their belongings
