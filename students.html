Student Assistant Program

Join us as a volunteer and gain valuable conference experience

FAPA 2026 invites pharmacy students to join our Student Assistant Program. Gain hands-on experience in organizing an international congress while networking with professionals from around the world.

Why Join as a Student Assistant?

Networking

Connect with pharmacy professionals and experts worldwide

Learning

Free access to all scientific sessions and exhibitions

Certificate

Receive official volunteer certificate and congress materials

Benefits

Meals, uniform, and social event access included

Responsibilities

  • Assist with participant registration and information desk
  • Guide attendees to session rooms and facilities
  • Support technical sessions and poster presentations
  • Help with social events and cultural programs
  • Provide language assistance (English/Thai)
  • Assist with general logistics and operations

Requirements

  • Current pharmacy student (undergraduate or graduate)
  • Good command of English (speaking and writing)
  • Enthusiastic, responsible, and team-oriented
  • Available for orientation session and full congress dates
  • Commitment to minimum 3 full days of service
  • Professional appearance and positive attitude

What You'll Receive

Free Registration

Full congress access including all scientific sessions

Meals Provided

Lunch and refreshments during your volunteer shifts

Official Uniform

Congress t-shirt and name badge

Congress Materials

Abstract book, program, and conference bag

Certificate

Official volunteer service certificate

Social Events

Invitation to welcome reception and cultural night
